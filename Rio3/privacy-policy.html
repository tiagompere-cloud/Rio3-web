Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Rio3 | Ozone Therapy ("Rio3," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect. We only collect the personal information you choose to provide through our contact or inquiry forms — typically your name, email address, phone number, and the contents of your message. We do not use analytics software, advertising pixels, or third-party tracking cookies on this website, and we do not build advertising profiles about visitors.

How we use your information. We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, to schedule and communicate about your care, and to provide the services you request.

Text messaging. If you provide your phone number and consent to be contacted by text, we may send you text messages related to your appointments and care. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. We do not share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing.

How we share information. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers. We may share information with service providers who help us operate (for example, our website host or our scheduling/payment provider) only as needed to provide our services, and with authorities where required by law.

Data security. We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect, consistent with the Florida Information Protection Act. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided by contacting us at the details below.

Children's privacy. This website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy. We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.
